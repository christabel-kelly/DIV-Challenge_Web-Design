About Us

Kalorm Training Insitute is an IT training institution that provides organizations with technological and professional training to develop the latest and best skills. With our highly skilled and qualified trainers, we provide our participants with excellent professional training in trending IT practices and tools.

We provide the participants with both formal and informal training that contribute immensely to individual growth and enhanced performance in in current or future jobs. Training activities and strategies are put in place to enable our participants keep abreast with new technologies, IT tools and concepts. Our training faciliates learning and development and promote the acquisition of knowledge, skills and abilities for effective job performance.
